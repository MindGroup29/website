Lifting Equipments

  1. Home
  2. Nos services
  3. Lifting Equipments

The teams at Meunier Lifting Equipment will accompany you in all of your development projects and in creating your handling and lifting equipment.

Our specialised team will provide you with on-board handling equipment for the naval sector:

  • SOLAS life-saving devices (boats and davits);
  • Purse line davits;
  • Cranes;
  • Launch and handling systems (ALP, ALM, ALS, SOLAS, etc.)
  • Winches;
  • Deck equipment.

We create custom-made equipment for the industry and for harbours:

  • Overhead cranes;
  • Spreader beams;
  • Purse line davits;
  • Winches.

Our experts are equipped to perform periodic inspections, to check the qualification of your means of lifting, and to perform maintenance.
